ABOUT MIWALLET

The company was officially registered in March, 2021

operating as Mi WALLET Private Limited Company

As eCommerce is growing bigger and bigger, businesses in Malawi face challenges when it comes to collecting payments online. Furthermore, A large number of people in Malawi are still using cash whenever they are making payments in retail and stores

This is where MIWALLET stepped in. A decision was made to develop a FinTech that would serve as An online payment gateway that accepts domestic & international payments. A service that would serve as unified mobile money wallet that does contactless payments on various POS services in shops and stores(NFC/QR)

Financial access for everyone
